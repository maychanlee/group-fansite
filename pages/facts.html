Discover Fun Facts About December Holidays!

Christmas

Did you know that Christmas wasn't always on December 25? Although Christmas celebrates the birth of Christ, it doesn’t actually mention December 25 anywhere in the bible. In fact, some historians think Jesus was born in the spring, not the winter at all! Christmas on December 25th wasn't celebrated until about 336 AD.

Hanukkah

Did you know that foods are fried in oil for Hanukkah to symbolize the oil that miraculously burned for eight nights during the rededication of the Jerusalem Temple in the 2nd century BCE, even though there was only enough oil for one day?
