FUN FACTS ABOUT HANUKKAH!

Did you know...

  1. Hanukkah is an eight-day Jewish festival of lights celebrating the rededication of the Second Temple of Jerusalem and commemorating a miracle where a small amount of oil for the temple lamp lasted for eight days.

  2. It begins on the 25th day of the Hebrew month of Kislev, which falls between late November and December on the Gregorian calendar.

  3. Key traditions include lighting a nine-branched candelabra called a hanukkiah, eating foods fried in oil like latkes and sufganiyot, and playing the dreidel game.
